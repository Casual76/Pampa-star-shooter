# Pampa Star Shooter Visual Bible

## Direction
- Arcade neon with readable silhouettes first, spectacle second.
- Dark arena planes, additive glows, hard graphic cores, minimal texture noise.
- Every combat object must read on a dark background at phone scale.

## Palette
- Neon Docks: cyan, ice blue, electric teal.
- Obsidian Relay: indigo, cold white, ion lavender.
- Amber Rift: amber, gold, warm orange.
- Danger and damage: coral red and magenta accents.

## Silhouette Rules
- Player ships use compact geometric cores with one dominant shape each.
- Fodder enemies stay circular or diamond-like.
- Tanks and brutes use wider silhouettes.
- Bosses get a crown or sigil layer, never only a bigger body.

## Glow Rules
- Core sprite stays crisp.
- Glow halo stays soft and wider than the sprite by roughly 20-40%.
- Use additive-looking bloom for bursts, pulses, dash trails, and pickup glows.
- Avoid full-screen bloom and muddy gradients.

## FX Rules
- Dash: stretched streak plus short after-image bloom.
- Pulse: ring wave with bright center spark and thin outer rim.
- Shield: clean ring with segmented energy ticks.
- Mine: radial burst with angular fragments.
- Hit spark: small, sharp, high-contrast cross or shard burst.
- Death bloom: larger layered burst with inner flash and outer smoke glow.

## UI Rules
- Typography is bold, mono-accented, and compact.
- Chips and panels keep hard contrast and bright accent edges.
- Motion is short and legible: pulse, readiness glow, entry slide, reward reveal.
- High contrast mode raises edge brightness and removes low-opacity muddiness.
